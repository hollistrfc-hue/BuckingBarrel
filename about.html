The Bucking Barrel is family-owned and family-run. Alysia built this place from the ground up — not to flip it or franchise it, but to give this corner of central Oklahoma somewhere worth showing up to.

Every detail of the venue, the bar, the arena, and the kitchen was designed by people who live here, work here, and want to see Perkins thrive. When you book with us, you're working directly with the people who own it.

We're opening Summer 2026. If you have questions or want to talk through an event, reach out directly — we actually answer.
